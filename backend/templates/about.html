Making Phones Safe Again

SafeIMEI is Nigeria's central database for reporting and verifying stolen mobile devices.

Our Mission

To stop the resale of stolen phones by making it impossible for thieves to sell them to unsuspecting buyers.

Our Vision

A transparent mobile market where every used phone comes with a verified history and peace of mind.

How We Protect You

  • 1
    Public Reporting Victims can report stolen phones directly on our site by uploading proof of ownership.
  • 2
    Police Verification Every report is reviewed by law enforcement before being added to the blacklist to prevent abuse.
  • 3
    Instant Search Buyers can check any IMEI for free. If it's flagged red, it's stolen. Simple as that.
